Dolphin

Dolphins range in size from the 1.7m long and 50kg

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Suborder: Whippomorpha
  • Order: Cetartiodactyla

Dolphins are a widely distributed and diverse group of aquatic mammals. They are an informal grouping within the order Cetacea, excluding whales and porpoises, so to zoologists the grouping is paraphyletic. (By wikipedia )
